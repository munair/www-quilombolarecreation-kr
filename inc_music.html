Berimbau Class

Join us weekdays at 7:00 PM (by RSVP only) for Berimbau Class. We will work various popular toques and sing Capoeira songs during this period. Class is 50 minutes long.

Atabaque Class

Join us weekends at 8:00 AM (by RSVP only) for Atabaque Class. We will work Samba, Barravento, or Congo in these sessions. Class is 80 minutes long.
