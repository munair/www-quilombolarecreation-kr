Privates

Learn Capoeira at your own pace and on a schedule that works for you. Contact Instrutor Zumbi via Facebook or call him on 010-7293-1777 to schedule Capoeira classes at a time most convenient for you.

Beginners

Join us every weekday evening at 7:30 PM for Capoeira fundamentals. In these sessions we train fundamental kicks and escapes, magical ginga steps, and a few other basic movements essential to expressing yourself in a Capoeira game. These movements look easy, but they are truly difficult to master. Mastery can take years, but no matter how long the journey takes, training Capoeira is always a fun and challenging workout filled with activities that make great and lasting friendships.

Attendance Restrictions
Class open to all levels (i.e. intermediate and advanced students may also attend these classes).

Intermediates

Join us every weekday evening at 8:30 PM for regular Capoeira classes. In these evening sessions we train sequencies and work with partners to improve our Capoeira vocabulary and reflexes.

Attendance Restrictions
Class open to intermediate and advanced students only (i.e. you need to be "baptized" or have at least one year of capoeira experience under your belt before you can attend these classes.)

Advanced

Join us weekends at 11:00 AM for advanced Capoeira training. In these mid-morning sessions we train advanced capoeira movement and concepts.

Attendance Restrictions
Class open to advanced students only. You need approval from the teacher to be able to attend these sessions. Contact Instrutor Zumbi via Facebook or call him on 010-7293-1777 to find out if you can attend.
